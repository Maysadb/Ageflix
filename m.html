Meet the Team

We are Grade 6A students—Ellen Alsahoree, Fawziah Almogherah, and Rouz Ahmed—working together to develop AGEFLIX. The idea was initiated by Ms. Angie Jamal, inspired by Netflix-style recommendations and the concerns of parents about age-appropriate content. The project was developed under her supervision and guidance. Our team collaborated to collect data, train the AI model using Teachable Machine, and test its performance, with each member contributing to building and presenting the project.
