About Ageflix Project

Ageflix is an innovative project that uses AI to detect the user's age and recommend movies suitable for their age group. We aimed to create a safe and personalized movie experience for kids, adults, and seniors. Our team worked collaboratively to design the interface, integrate Teachable Machine for age detection, and build an interactive platform similar to Netflix.
